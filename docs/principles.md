# Research Principles

Two foundational principle sets guide research and writing in this project.

## Research Strategy Principles

8 Carlini-inspired principles for evaluating research ideas and managing project strategy:

- **RS1–RS8**: Novelty, comparative advantage, conclusion-first test, riskiest assumptions, scooping risk, triage, cross-field bridges, and impact forecasting.

Used by the research companion (ideation and evaluation) and research strategist (project-level decisions).

## Academic Writing Principles

30 prose-quality principles organized into 6 categories:

- **Clarity**: Say exactly what you mean
- **Structure**: Build a skeleton your reader can follow
- **Evidence**: Back up every claim
- **Precision**: Make arguments airtight
- **Audience**: Write for people who will skim
- **Revision**: Polish until it shines
- **Fidelity**: No extrapolation beyond sources

Used by the paper-read skill (critical reading) and all writing-adjacent workflows.
